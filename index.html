What are the login hours?

Our platform is accessible 24/7, allowing users to log in and place orders at any time that suits their schedule. Whether it's a late-night craving or a lunchtime treat, our doors are always open to serve you delicious meals with just a few clicks.

What do i get my refund?

In the Food-Rush-Online-Delivery project, your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're ever dissatisfied with your order, we've got you covered with our refund policy. Simply reach out to our customer service team, and we'll swiftly process your refund, ensuring a hassle-free experience. How long it will take food to arrive?

We put efficiency and speed first in the Food-Rush-Online-Delivery initiative to make sure your food gets to you on time. Various factors, including geography, order volume, and current demand, might affect delivery timeframes. For local deliveries, we typically aim for an estimated delivery window of 30 to 45 minutes. We always try to fulfill items within a fair timeline. However, delivery times may be a little longer at busy seasons or in bad weather. You may be confident that our committed staff will stop at nothing to reduce wait times and make sure your mouthwatering Chicken Pepperoni meal gets to you as soon as possible without sacrificing quality.

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, absolutely! At the Food-Rush-Online-Delivery project, we cater to diverse tastes and preferences by offering a wide range of options, including both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Whether you're craving a hearty veggie pizza loaded with fresh toppings or a flavorful non-veg option like Chicken Pepperoni pizza, we have something delicious for everyone. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive dining experience that satisfies all appetites, ensuring that every customer finds something they love on our menu.?
